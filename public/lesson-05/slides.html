What IS a Website? 🤔

Every website is made of 3 building blocks:

🏗️
HTML
The structure & content
Like the skeleton of a building
🎨
CSS
The style & colors
Like paint, wallpaper & furniture
⚡
JavaScript
The behavior & actions
Like electricity & plumbing

You don't need to learn any of these — AI will write them all for you!

Anatomy of a Web Page 🧬

🏠 Header Your name or site title, logo, welcome message
🗺️ Navigation Links to jump between sections (Home, About, Contact)
📝 Content The main stuff! Text, images, cards, galleries, forms...
📍 Footer Copyright, social links, "Made by..." credits

Almost every website follows this pattern!

How to Describe a Website to AI 💬

Go section by section:

  • 🏠 Header — “A big welcome banner with my name and a gradient background”
  • 👤 About — “A section with my photo on the left and bio on the right”
  • 🎨 Content — “A grid of 4 hobby cards with icons and descriptions”
  • 🎨 Colors — “Use blue and white with rounded corners everywhere”
  • 📍 Footer — “A dark footer with my name and social links”

4 Design Principles 🎨

Real designers follow these rules:

🎯
Contrast
Make important things stand out with different sizes & colors
📐
Alignment
Line things up neatly — nothing looks randomly placed
🌬️
Whitespace
Give things room to breathe — don't cram everything
🔄
Consistency
Same fonts, colors, and styles throughout

Good vs Bad Descriptions 🔍

❌ Too Vague
“Make me a website”
“I want a cool page”
“Build something nice”
✅ Section by Section
“A portfolio with a blue header, About Me section with my photo, and 4 hobby cards in a grid”
“A fan page about cats with fun facts, a top 5 list, and purple-gold colors”

The more sections & details you describe, the better your website!
