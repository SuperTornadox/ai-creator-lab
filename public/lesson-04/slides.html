Key Tip: Match the Mood 🎯

Your image prompts should match the MOOD of your story.
Adventure Story
→ Exciting, dynamic, action-packed images
Calm Story
→ Soft, peaceful, gentle images

Good Match ✅

Story Text
“The brave knight charged into the dragon’s cave, sword gleaming in the firelight...”
⚔️🔥
Image Prompt
A knight with a glowing sword entering a dramatic fire-lit dragon cave, epic adventure scene
Exciting matches exciting!

Bad Match ❌

Funny Story
“Mr. Whiskers the cat accidentally knocked over every plant in the house and pretended nothing happened...”
😱
Image Result
Scary, dark, horror-style cat image
Wait, that doesn’t feel right!

Funny story needs funny images 😸
