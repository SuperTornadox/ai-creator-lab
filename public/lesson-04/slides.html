How to Describe a Game to AI

  • ✅ Rules — “3 lives, 30-second timer”
  • ✅ Controls — “move with arrow keys”
  • ✅ Scoring — “get +10 for each catch”
  • ✅ Look & feel — “bright colors, fun for kids”
  • ❌ Avoid vague — “make something cool” 🚫

The more details you give, the better the game!
