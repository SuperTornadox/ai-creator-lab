What Is a "Prompt"?

A prompt is your instructions to AI.

🍳 Think of it like a recipe:
"Make me food" → ??? (raw potato?)
"Make me a chocolate cake with strawberries on top" → 🍰

Vague vs. Specific Prompts

❌ Vague
"Write me a story"

Result: generic, boring story about a kid going to school

✅ Specific
"Write a funny story about a shy dragon who accidentally gets superpowers in a candy world"

Result: creative, detailed, exactly what you wanted!

Good Prompt =

  • 🎯 Clear — AI knows exactly what you want
  • 🔍 Specific — include details and examples
  • ✨ Creative — interesting ideas get interesting results

Better instructions → better results. You're the director!
