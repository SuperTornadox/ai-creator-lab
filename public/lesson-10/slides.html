Quality Checklist ✅

Before you call it done, ask yourself:

  • 👁️ Does it look good? — Is the layout clean and organized?
  • 📖 Does it read well? — Is the text interesting and clear?
  • 🖼️ Do images match? — Do visuals fit your content?
  • ⚙️ Does it work? — Does the page display correctly?
  • 🌟 Would you show it off? — Are you proud of it?
