Games vs Apps 🎮 📱

🎮 Games

- Fun to play
- Entertainment
- Made for fun
- You play them

📱 Apps

- Solve a problem
- Save time or effort
- Made to be useful
- You USE them

Both are programs — but apps solve real problems!

How to Describe an App to AI 💬

  • 📋 Features — What should the app DO?
  • 🖼️ Layout — Where do things go on screen?
  • 🔄 Behavior — What happens when you click/type?
  • 💾 Data — What needs to be saved or remembered?
The more specific your description, the better the AI builds it!
"Make a calculator" → okay
"Make a calculator with history, dark theme, and keyboard input" → great!

User Testing 🧑‍💻

Watch someone ELSE use your app.

  • 👀 Watch silently — Don't tell them what to do
  • 🤔 Notice confusion — Where do they hesitate?
  • ❓ Ask questions — "What did you expect to happen?"
  • 📝 Take notes — Use feedback to improve

If they need your help to use it, the app needs improvement!
