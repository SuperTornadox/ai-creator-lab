Same Question, Different Answers 💬

Question: "Why is the sky blue?"

📚 Helpful Tutor
"Great question! Sunlight is made of all colors. When it hits the atmosphere, blue light gets scattered the most because it travels in shorter waves. That's called Rayleigh scattering!"
🏴‍☠️ Pirate Talk Comedian
"Arr matey! The sky be blue 'cause the ocean be so vain, it convinced the whole sky to match its outfit! Shiver me timbers! But really, it's all about light gettin' scattered, savvy?"

The system prompt changes everything!

Conversation Design Tips 💡

  • 👋 Welcome message — greet the user right away
  • ❓ Suggested questions — help users know what to ask
  • 🎭 Stay in character — your bot should be consistent
  • 📏 Keep it short — nobody wants to read a novel
  • 😀 Be friendly — even error messages can be fun
