What's a Prompt?

A prompt is your INSTRUCTIONS to AI

🍳 Think of it as a recipe:
the clearer your recipe, the better the food!

Vague vs. Specific

❌ Vague prompt
"Write me a story"
🥱 Boring, generic, short... meh
✅ Specific prompt
"Write a funny story about a shy dragon in candy world who accidentally gets superpowers"
🤩 Vivid, creative, hilarious, unique!
