Tips for Great Scene Images 🎨

  • ✅ Be specific — “a golden sunset over a mountain lake” beats “nature”
  • ✅ Include mood — “mysterious dark forest” or “bright cheerful kitchen”
  • ✅ Mention characters — “a brave kid in a red cape”
  • ✅ Think cinematic — imagine it on a movie screen!
