# Lesson 12: Final Project — Polish & Present / Teacher Script
# 第十二课：毕业项目 — 润色与展示 / 教师逐字稿

**Duration / 时长**: 40 minutes / 分钟
**Materials / 材料**: Slides (`slides.html`), Interactive webpage (`index.html`), Student laptops
**Goal / 目标**: Students add final touches to their projects, prepare and deliver presentations, and celebrate completing the course.
**目标**: 学生为项目添加最后的修饰，准备并进行演示，庆祝完成课程。

---

## Pre-Class Preparation / 课前准备

> **Important / 重要:**
> - This is the final class — make it feel special and celebratory
> - Prepare any optional "graduation" touches: certificates, stickers, or a simple printed certificate
> - Test the Presentation Mode feature on the page (the "Launch Presentation Mode" button)
> - Plan the presentation order — with 2 students, each gets about 5-7 minutes to present plus Q&A
> - Consider connecting a student's laptop to a projector/screen for presentations
> - Have a camera or phone ready if you want to take photos of presentations (with appropriate permissions)
>
> 课前准备：
> - 这是最后一堂课——让它有特别和庆祝的感觉
> - 准备任何可选的"毕业"元素：证书、贴纸或简单的打印证书
> - 测试页面上的演示模式功能（"Launch Presentation Mode"按钮）
> - 计划演示顺序——2个学生每人约有5-7分钟演示加问答
> - 考虑将学生的笔记本电脑连接到投影仪/屏幕进行演示
> - 准备好相机或手机以便拍摄演示照片（获得适当许可）

---

## Final Polish / 最后润色 (20 minutes / 分钟)

### [SLIDE 1 — Title] *(0:00)*

EN: "Welcome to our FINAL class of AI Creator Lab! This is it — presentation day. We're going to spend the first 20 minutes doing final polish on your projects, then we'll have formal presentations, and then we'll celebrate!"

CN: "欢迎来到 AI 创造者实验室的最后一堂课！就是今天——演示日。我们将花前20分钟做项目的最后润色，然后进行正式演示，然后我们庆祝！"

---

### [SLIDE 2 — Polish Checklist] *(0:01)*

EN: "Here's your final polish checklist. Review everything one more time — read through your content, check your images, make sure everything works. Fix any typos or rough edges. Add any finishing touches that would make it better. And most importantly — SAVE your final version!"

CN: "这是你的最终润色清单。再检查一遍所有东西——通读你的内容，检查图片，确保一切正常。修正任何拼写错误或粗糙的地方。添加任何能让它更好的最后修饰。最重要的是——保存你的最终版本！"

---

### [SLIDE 3 — Presentation Tips] *(0:02)*

EN: "While you're polishing, start thinking about your presentation. Three tips: Speak clearly — slow down and use a confident voice. Show your work — walk us through your project on the screen. And explain your process — tell us HOW you made it with AI. What tools did you use? What prompts worked best?"

CN: "在你润色的同时，开始想你的演示。三个提示：说清楚——放慢速度，用自信的声音说话。展示你的作品——在屏幕上带我们看你的项目。解释你的过程——告诉我们你是如何用 AI 做的。你用了什么工具？什么提示词效果最好？"

---

### [SLIDE 4 — Presentation Template] *(0:03)*

EN: "Here's a simple structure for your presentation. Start with: 'My project is...' — tell us what it is. Then: 'I made it using...' — which AI tools did you use? Then: 'The hardest part was...' — what challenges did you face? And finally: 'I learned that...' — what's the biggest thing you discovered?"

CN: "这是你演示的简单结构。开始说：'我的项目是……'——告诉我们它是什么。然后：'我使用了……来做'——你用了哪些 AI 工具？然后：'最难的部分是……'——你面临了什么挑战？最后：'我学到了……'——你发现的最大收获是什么？"

EN: "Go ahead and fill in the 'About My Project' and 'Creative Process' sections on the lesson page. And try clicking the 'Launch Presentation Mode' button to see how it looks!"

CN: "继续在课程页面上填写'About My Project'和'Creative Process'部分。然后试试点击'Launch Presentation Mode'按钮看看效果！"

---

### Polish Work Time *(0:04–0:20)*

**[Switch from slides to monitoring student screens / 从幻灯片切换到巡视学生屏幕]**

EN: "You have about 16 minutes for final polish and presentation prep. Use the tools at the top if you need to make any last changes. Then fill in your presentation notes. I'll come around to check on each of you."

CN: "你有大约16分钟做最后润色和演示准备。如果需要做最后的修改，使用顶部的工具。然后填写你的演示笔记。我会过来看你们每个人。"

**[Walk between students / 在学生之间走动]**

> **For each student, check / 对每个学生，检查:**
> EN:
> 1. Is the project in a presentable state?
> 2. Have they written their presentation notes (About + Process)?
> 3. Have they tried Presentation Mode?
> 4. Do they feel ready? Any last-minute help needed?
>
> CN:
> 1. 项目是否处于可展示的状态？
> 2. 他们写了演示笔记（关于+过程）吗？
> 3. 他们试过演示模式了吗？
> 4. 他们觉得准备好了吗？需要最后的帮助吗？

> **Contingency / 应对方案:**
> EN: If a student's project isn't quite done: "That's completely okay! Show what you HAVE done. Even an unfinished project shows a lot of learning. Focus your presentation on what you made and what you would add if you had more time."
> CN: 如果学生的项目没有完全完成："这完全没关系！展示你已经完成的部分。即使是未完成的项目也展示了很多学习。把演示重点放在你做了什么以及如果有更多时间你会添加什么。"

> **At 0:15, give a 5-minute warning / 在 0:15 给出 5 分钟提醒:**
> EN: "Five minutes left for polish! Make sure your presentation notes are filled in. We'll start presentations soon."
> CN: "润色还有五分钟！确保你的演示笔记已经填好。我们很快开始演示。"

> **At 0:18, give a 2-minute warning / 在 0:18 给出 2 分钟提醒:**
> EN: "Two minutes! Finish up, save everything, and get ready to present."
> CN: "还有两分钟！完成收尾，保存所有东西，准备好演示。"

---

## Formal Presentations / 正式演示 (15 minutes / 分钟) *(0:20–0:35)*

### [SLIDE 5 — It's Showtime!] *(0:20)*

EN: "It's showtime! Time for presentations. Remember to be a great audience — listen carefully, watch the project, and give encouraging feedback after each presentation."

CN: "登台时间到！演示开始。记住做一个好观众——认真听，观看项目，在每次演示后给出鼓励的反馈。"

EN: "Who wants to go first?"

CN: "谁想先来？"

**[Select presentation order / 选择演示顺序]**

---

### Student 1 Presentation *(~7 min)*

EN: "The floor is yours! You can use Presentation Mode on the page, or just show us your project directly. Start by telling us: what is your project?"

CN: "你的舞台！你可以使用页面上的演示模式，或者直接给我们展示你的项目。先告诉我们：你的项目是什么？"

**[Student presents — let them talk without interruption / 学生演示——让他们不被打断地说]**

**[After they finish / 他们完成后:]**

EN: "Great job! Let me ask you a couple of questions..."

CN: "做得好！让我问你几个问题……"

**Suggested questions / 建议问题:**
- EN: "Which AI tool was most useful for your project?" / CN: "哪个 AI 工具对你的项目最有用？"
- EN: "If you had one more class to work on this, what would you add?" / CN: "如果你还有一节课来做这个，你会添加什么？"
- EN: "What was the most surprising thing AI generated for you?" / CN: "AI 为你生成的最令人惊讶的东西是什么？"

**[To the other student:]**

EN: "Do you have any questions or comments for them?"

CN: "你对他/她有什么问题或评论吗？"

---

### Student 2 Presentation *(~7 min)*

EN: "Your turn! Show us what you've built!"

CN: "轮到你了！给我们展示你构建的东西！"

**[Student presents / 学生演示]**

**[After they finish — ask questions, invite peer feedback / 完成后——提问，邀请同伴反馈]**

---

### Presentation Wrap-up *(~1 min)*

EN: "Both of you did an incredible job — not just on the presentations, but on the projects themselves. You planned, you built, you problem-solved, and you presented like pros."

CN: "你们俩都做得非常棒——不仅是演示，项目本身也是。你们规划了，构建了，解决了问题，像专业人士一样展示了。"

---

## Course Review & Graduation / 课程回顾和毕业 (5 minutes / 分钟) *(0:35–0:40)*

### [SLIDE 6 — Course Recap] *(0:35)*

EN: "Let's take a moment to look back at everything we learned in AI Creator Lab. Twelve lessons! We started with basic AI chat in Lesson 1 — just learning how to talk to AI. Then we wrote stories, created art, made picture books. We composed music, directed films. We learned to code and make games. We even explored AI ethics and learned to think critically about AI."

CN: "让我们花点时间回顾我们在 AI 创造者实验室学到的一切。十二节课！我们从第1课的基础 AI 聊天开始——只是学习如何和 AI 对话。然后我们写故事、创作艺术、制作绘本。我们作曲、导演电影。我们学会了编程和制作游戏。我们甚至探索了 AI 伦理，学会了批判性地思考 AI。"

EN: "And then for these last three lessons, you put it ALL together into your own original project. That's amazing."

CN: "然后在最后三节课里，你们把所有东西都结合到了自己的原创项目中。这真的很了不起。"

---

### [SLIDE 7 — What's Next] *(0:37)*

EN: "So what's next? AI is changing fast. New tools come out every month. But the skills you've learned here — writing good prompts, thinking creatively, combining different tools, and being critical about what AI produces — those skills will help you with ANY new AI tool that comes along."

CN: "那接下来呢？AI 变化很快。每个月都有新工具出现。但你在这里学到的技能——写好提示词、创造性思考、组合不同工具、批判性地看待 AI 生成的内容——这些技能将帮助你使用任何新出现的 AI 工具。"

EN: "Keep chatting with AI, keep creating art, keep coding, keep making music. And always remember to think critically about what AI gives you. Share your creations with your family and friends!"

CN: "继续和 AI 聊天，继续创作艺术，继续编程，继续制作音乐。永远记得批判性地看待 AI 给你的东西。和你的家人朋友分享你的创作！"

---

### [SLIDE 8 — Congratulations!] *(0:38)*

EN: "And now... the moment you've been waiting for."

CN: "现在……你们期待的时刻。"

**[Pause for dramatic effect / 停顿增加戏剧效果]**

EN: "Congratulations! You are officially AI Creators! You completed the entire AI Creator Lab course. You started as beginners and now you can use AI to create text, images, music, films, code, and games. I'm really, really proud of both of you."

CN: "恭喜！你们正式成为 AI 创造者了！你们完成了整个 AI 创造者实验室课程。你们从初学者开始，现在可以用 AI 创造文字、图片、音乐、电影、代码和游戏。我真的非常非常为你们俩感到骄傲。"

**[If you have certificates or small gifts, distribute them now / 如果你有证书或小礼物，现在发放]**

EN: "Remember — AI is a powerful tool, and YOU are the creative force behind it. Keep creating, keep learning, and keep being awesome!"

CN: "记住——AI 是强大的工具，而你才是背后的创造力量。继续创造，继续学习，继续做了不起的自己！"

EN: "Thank you for being such amazing students. This has been a wonderful class, and I hope you had as much fun as I did!"

CN: "谢谢你们做了这么棒的学生。这是一门很棒的课程，我希望你们和我一样享受它！"
