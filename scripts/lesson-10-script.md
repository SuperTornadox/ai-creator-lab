# Lesson 10: Final Project — Planning / Teacher Script
# 第十课：毕业项目 — 规划 / 教师逐字稿

**Duration / 时长**: 40 minutes / 分钟
**Materials / 材料**: Slides (`slides.html`), Interactive webpage (`index.html`), Student laptops
**Goal / 目标**: Students review all the AI skills they have learned, choose a final project type, write a detailed plan, and begin collecting materials.
**目标**: 学生回顾所有学过的 AI 技能，选择毕业项目类型，撰写详细计划，并开始收集素材。

---

## Pre-Class Preparation / 课前准备

> **Important / 重要:**
> - Make sure the Lesson 10 interactive page is working
> - Review students' previous work from Lessons 1-9 to reference during the recap
> - Prepare to help students scope their projects realistically for 2 class sessions
> - Consider each student's strengths — some may gravitate toward writing, others toward visuals or code
>
> 课前准备：
> - 确保第10课互动页面正常运行
> - 回顾学生在第1-9课的之前作品，以便在回顾中引用
> - 准备帮助学生将项目范围控制在2节课时间内
> - 考虑每个学生的优势——有些可能偏向写作，有些偏向视觉或代码

---

## Skills Recap / 技能回顾 (5 minutes / 分钟)

### [SLIDE 1 — Title] *(0:00)*

EN: "Welcome to a very special set of lessons! For the next three classes, you're going to work on your FINAL PROJECT. This is your chance to take everything you've learned and build something amazing."

CN: "欢迎来到一系列非常特别的课程！在接下来的三节课里，你将完成你的毕业项目。这是你把学到的所有东西结合起来，创造出令人惊叹的作品的机会。"

---

### [SLIDE 2 — Look How Far You've Come] *(0:01)*

EN: "Before we start planning, let's take a moment to look at how much you've learned. When you walked into this class for the first time, most of you had never really used AI tools. Now look at you — you've chatted with AI characters, written stories, created art, made music, directed films, built programs, designed games, and even learned about AI's limitations."

CN: "在开始规划之前，让我们花点时间看看你们学了多少东西。当你们第一次走进这个课堂时，大多数人从来没有真正使用过 AI 工具。现在看看你们——你们和 AI 角色聊过天、写过故事、创作过艺术、制作过音乐、导演过电影、编写过程序、设计过游戏，甚至了解了 AI 的局限性。"

EN: "That's a LOT. You should be proud of how far you've come."

CN: "这真的很多。你们应该为自己的进步感到自豪。"

---

### [SLIDE 3 — Skills Unlocked] *(0:02)*

EN: "Here's a quick recap of all the skills you've unlocked. Text AI — you learned to write prompts and have conversations with AI. Image AI — you can create any visual you can describe. Music AI — you made original songs. Film AI — you created multi-scene mini films. Code AI — you built interactive pages and programs. Game AI — you designed and prototyped games."

CN: "这是你们解锁的所有技能的快速回顾。文字 AI——你学会了写提示词并与 AI 对话。图像 AI——你可以创造任何你能描述的画面。音乐 AI——你制作了原创歌曲。电影 AI——你创建了多场景迷你电影。代码 AI——你构建了互动页面和程序。游戏 AI——你设计并制作了游戏原型。"

EN: "Your final project can use ANY combination of these skills."

CN: "你的毕业项目可以使用这些技能的任意组合。"

---

## Project Options / 项目选项 (10 minutes / 分钟)

### [SLIDE 4 — Choose Your Project] *(0:03)*

EN: "You have four project options. Option 1: AI Magazine — create a multi-page digital magazine with articles and AI-generated illustrations. Think of it like creating your own Highlights or National Geographic for Kids."

CN: "你有四个项目选项。选项1：AI 杂志——创建一本有文章和 AI 生成插图的多页数字杂志。想象一下创建你自己的 Highlights 或国家地理少儿版。"

EN: "Option 2: AI Short Film — a longer, more polished version of what you did in Lesson 6. Multiple scenes, maybe narration, a real storyline."

CN: "选项2：AI 短片——你在第6课做的东西的更长、更精致的版本。多个场景，也许有旁白，一个真实的故事线。"

EN: "Option 3: AI Game — a more complex game than you built in Lesson 8. Maybe it has multiple levels, or different endings, or special features."

CN: "选项3：AI 游戏——比你在第8课制作的更复杂的游戏。也许它有多个关卡，或不同的结局，或特殊功能。"

EN: "Option 4: Free Choice — combine ANY tools to create something totally unique. Maybe it's an interactive story with images and music. Maybe it's an AI art gallery with descriptions. The sky is the limit."

CN: "选项4：自由选择——组合任何工具来创造完全独特的东西。也许是一个带有图片和音乐的互动故事。也许是一个有描述的 AI 艺术画廊。无限可能。"

**[Pause for questions / 暂停回答问题]**

EN: "Any questions about the options? Remember — there's no wrong choice. Pick the one that excites you the most."

CN: "对这些选项有什么问题吗？记住——没有错误的选择。选最让你兴奋的那个。"

---

### [SLIDE 5 — What Makes a Great Project] *(0:06)*

EN: "What makes a great final project? It's not about being the biggest or the most complicated. It's about four things: Creativity — your unique ideas and personal style. Effort — the time and care you put into it. Personal style — it should feel like YOU made it, not just an AI. And using AI tools — show off what you've learned!"

CN: "什么样的毕业项目才是好项目？不是最大或最复杂的。而是四个方面：创意——你独特的想法和个人风格。努力——你投入的时间和心血。个人风格——应该能感觉到是你做的，不只是 AI。还有使用 AI 工具——展示你学到的东西！"

---

### [SLIDE 6 — Planning Template] *(0:07)*

EN: "Every great project starts with a plan. On the lesson page, you'll fill out: a title — give your project a cool name. A description — what is it about? Which tools you'll use. And a step-by-step plan — how will you build it? Break it into small pieces."

CN: "每个伟大的项目都从计划开始。在课程页面上，你将填写：一个标题——给你的项目起个酷名字。描述——它是关于什么的？你将使用哪些工具。以及分步计划——你将如何构建它？把它分成小块。"

---

### [SLIDE 7 — Tips] *(0:08)*

EN: "Three important tips. First: start small, then add more. Don't try to build everything at once. Get the basic version working first, then make it better. Second: iterate — that means improve as you go. Your first version doesn't have to be perfect. Third: ask for help. If you're stuck, talk to me or brainstorm with the AI advisor on the page."

CN: "三个重要提示。第一：从小处开始，然后添加更多。不要试图一次构建所有东西。先让基础版本运行起来，然后再让它更好。第二：迭代——意思是边做边改进。你的第一个版本不必完美。第三：寻求帮助。如果你卡住了，跟我说话或者和页面上的 AI 顾问讨论。"

---

### [SLIDE 8 — Let's Plan!] *(0:09)*

EN: "Alright, open your laptops and go to the Lesson 10 page. First, choose your project type by clicking one of the four cards. Then scroll down and fill out your plan. Use the AI brainstorm chat if you need help coming up with ideas. And if you finish your plan early, start collecting materials — generate some images or draft some text!"

CN: "好，打开电脑，进入第10课页面。首先，点击四张卡片之一选择你的项目类型。然后向下滚动填写你的计划。如果需要帮助想出点子，就用 AI 头脑风暴聊天。如果你提前完成了计划，就开始收集素材——生成一些图片或起草一些文字！"

---

## Hands-on Planning / 动手规划 (25 minutes / 分钟)

### Phase 1: Choose Project Type (5 min) *(0:10–0:15)*

**[Switch from slides to monitoring student screens / 从幻灯片切换到巡视学生屏幕]**

EN: "Take a few minutes to think about which project type you want. Look at the four options. Think about what you enjoyed most in our earlier lessons. If you loved making stories and art, maybe the Magazine is for you. If you loved the film lesson, go with the Short Film. If coding was your favorite, try the Game. And if you want to mix everything, pick Free Choice."

CN: "花几分钟想想你想要哪种项目类型。看看四个选项。想想你在之前的课程中最喜欢什么。如果你喜欢写故事和做艺术，也许杂志适合你。如果你喜欢电影课，选短片。如果编程是你最喜欢的，试试游戏。如果你想把所有东西混在一起，选自由选择。"

**[Give students 3–5 minutes to decide / 给学生 3-5 分钟决定]**

> **Contingency / 应对方案:**
> EN: If a student can't decide: "What was your favorite lesson? What project are you most excited to show your family? Start there."
> CN: 如果学生无法决定："你最喜欢哪节课？你最想向家人展示什么项目？从那里开始。"

---

### Phase 2: Write the Plan (10 min) *(0:15–0:25)*

EN: "Great, now scroll down to the planning form. Fill in your project title — make it catchy! Then write a description of what your project will be about. Check the tools you plan to use. And write out your step-by-step plan."

CN: "很好，现在向下滚动到规划表。填写你的项目标题——让它醒目！然后写一个关于你的项目将是什么的描述。勾选你计划使用的工具。然后写出你的分步计划。"

EN: "Think about it this way: if you had to explain your project to someone who's never seen it, what would you say?"

CN: "这样想：如果你必须向一个从未见过你项目的人解释它，你会怎么说？"

**[Give students 10 minutes / 给学生 10 分钟]**

**[Walk around and review plans as students write / 走动并在学生写作时审查计划]**

> **Teacher guidance / 教师指导:**
> EN: As you review each student's plan, look for: Is the scope realistic for 2 class sessions? Are the steps concrete enough? Does the plan use at least 2 AI tools? Help them scope down if their idea is too ambitious, or add detail if the plan is too vague.
> CN: 审查每个学生的计划时注意：范围对2节课时间是否现实？步骤是否足够具体？计划是否使用了至少2种 AI 工具？如果想法太大帮他们缩小范围，如果计划太模糊帮他们添加细节。

> **Contingency / 应对方案:**
> EN: If a student's plan is too ambitious: "I love the ambition! But we only have about 80 minutes of building time total. Let's figure out the core version — what's the minimum that would still be awesome? You can always add more features if you have time."
> CN: 如果学生的计划太大："我喜欢这个抱负！但我们总共只有大约80分钟的构建时间。让我们想想核心版本——最少做到什么样仍然很棒？如果有时间你随时可以添加更多功能。"

---

### Phase 3: Brainstorm & Collect Materials (10 min) *(0:25–0:35)*

EN: "If you've finished your plan — great job! Click 'Save My Plan' to save it. Now scroll down to the brainstorm section. You can chat with the AI project advisor to refine your ideas. And you can start generating images or thinking about the content you'll need."

CN: "如果你完成了计划——做得好！点击'Save My Plan'保存。现在向下滚动到头脑风暴部分。你可以和 AI 项目顾问聊天来完善你的想法。你也可以开始生成图片或思考你需要的内容。"

EN: "Remember: anything you create today is a head start for next class, which is all about BUILDING. The more you prepare now, the more time you'll have for building next time."

CN: "记住：你今天创造的任何东西都是下节课的起步，下节课全部是关于构建的。你现在准备得越多，下次构建的时间就越多。"

**[Give students 10 minutes / 给学生 10 分钟]**

> **Contingency / 应对方案:**
> EN: If a student finishes everything early: "Amazing! Try generating a few different images in different styles to see what looks best for your project. Or start drafting your first piece of content."
> CN: 如果学生提前完成所有事情："太棒了！试着用不同风格生成几张不同的图片，看看哪种最适合你的项目。或者开始起草你的第一段内容。"

---

## Wrap-up / 课程总结 (5 minutes / 分钟) *(0:35–0:40)*

EN: "Alright everyone, let's pause. Make sure you've saved your plan! Click the 'Save My Plan' button if you haven't."

CN: "好了大家，让我们暂停。确保你保存了你的计划！如果还没有，点击'Save My Plan'按钮。"

**[Wait for students to save / 等待学生保存]**

EN: "Let's do a quick share. Can each of you tell me in one sentence: what is your project, and which type did you choose?"

CN: "让我们快速分享一下。你们每个人能用一句话告诉我：你的项目是什么，你选了哪种类型？"

**[Student 1 shares / 学生1分享]**

**[Student 2 shares / 学生2分享]**

EN: "Those both sound awesome! Next class is BUILD DAY — you'll have the entire class to bring your project to life. All the AI tools will be right there on the page for you. Come ready to create!"

CN: "都听起来很棒！下节课是构建日——你将有整节课的时间把你的项目变为现实。所有的 AI 工具都会在页面上为你准备好。准备好来创造吧！"
