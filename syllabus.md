# AI Creator Lab — Course Syllabus
# AI 创造者实验室 — 课程大纲

**Grade Level / 年级**: 6-7 (Middle School)
**Duration / 课时**: 12 weeks, 1 session per week, 40-45 minutes each
**Class Size / 班级人数**: 2 students
**Prerequisites / 前置要求**: None — no programming or AI experience needed
**Language / 语言**: English instruction with Chinese support

---

## Course Description / 课程简介

AI Creator Lab is a hands-on creative course where students learn to use artificial intelligence as a creative tool. Through 12 weekly sessions, students will chat with AI, write stories, create artwork, compose music, build interactive programs, design games, and explore AI ethics — all while developing critical thinking skills about technology.

AI 创造者实验室是一门以动手实践为核心的创意课程，学生将学习把人工智能作为创作工具。在 12 周的课程中，学生将与 AI 对话、写故事、创作艺术品、作曲、构建交互式程序、设计游戏，并探索 AI 伦理——同时培养对技术的批判性思维能力。

---

## Learning Objectives / 学习目标

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

1. **Communicate effectively with AI** — Write clear, specific prompts to get desired outputs
2. **Create with multiple AI tools** — Use text, image, music, and code generation AI
3. **Think critically about AI** — Understand AI's capabilities, limitations, and ethical implications
4. **Iterate and improve** — Use feedback loops to refine AI-generated content
5. **Combine tools creatively** — Integrate multiple AI tools into a cohesive project
6. **Present their work** — Explain their creative process and showcase their projects

完成本课程后，学生将能够：
1. **与 AI 有效沟通** — 撰写清晰、具体的提示词以获得所需输出
2. **使用多种 AI 工具创作** — 使用文本、图像、音乐和代码生成 AI
3. **批判性地思考 AI** — 理解 AI 的能力、局限性和伦理含义
4. **迭代和改进** — 使用反馈循环来完善 AI 生成的内容
5. **创造性地组合工具** — 将多种 AI 工具整合到一个连贯的项目中
6. **展示作品** — 解释创作过程并展示项目

## Weekly Schedule / 每周课程安排

### Phase 1: Foundations / 基础阶段 (Weeks 1-3)

| Week | Lesson | Topic | Project Output |
|------|--------|-------|---------------|
| 1 | AI First Contact | Introduction to AI, first conversation | AI character interview article |
| 2 | AI Story Factory | Prompt engineering, creative writing | A creative short story |
| 3 | AI Artist | Image generation, visual descriptions | 3-5 AI-generated artworks |

### Phase 2: Expanding Skills / 技能扩展阶段 (Weeks 4-6)

| Week | Lesson | Topic | Project Output |
|------|--------|-------|---------------|
| 4 | AI Picture Book | Combining text + image AI | A 4-6 page illustrated book |
| 5 | AI Musician | Music generation, creative direction | An original AI song |

### Phase 3: Code & Ethics / 编程与伦理阶段 (Weeks 7-9)

| Week | Lesson | Topic | Project Output |
|------|--------|-------|---------------|
| 7 | AI Coder (Part 1) | AI-assisted coding basics | A simple interactive program |
| 8 | AI Game Designer | Game design with AI | A playable web mini-game |
| 9 | AI Truth Challenge | AI ethics, deepfakes, bias | Ethics challenge score + discussion |

### Phase 4: Final Project / 毕业项目阶段 (Weeks 10-12)

| Week | Lesson | Topic | Project Output |
|------|--------|-------|---------------|
| 10 | Final Project — Planning | Project selection and planning | Project plan + initial materials |
| 11 | Final Project — Building | Full build session | Main project body |
| 12 | Final Project — Present | Polish and presentations | Completed project + presentation |

## Assessment / 评估方式

This course uses project-based assessment rather than traditional tests:

本课程采用项目制评估而非传统考试：

- **Weekly Projects (70%)**: Each lesson produces a creative work. Assessment is based on effort, creativity, and iterative improvement — not perfection.
- **Participation & Discussion (10%)**: Engagement during warm-ups, sharing work, and contributing to discussions (especially during the ethics lesson).
- **Final Project (20%)**: A capstone project combining multiple AI tools, presented to the class in Week 12.

**每周项目（70%）**：每节课产出一件创意作品。评估基于努力程度、创造力和迭代改进——而非完美程度。
**参与和讨论（10%）**：在热身环节、作品分享和讨论中的参与度（尤其是伦理课程）。
**毕业项目（20%）**：结合多种 AI 工具的综合项目，在第 12 周向全班展示。

## Course Policies / 课程规则

### AI Usage Guidelines / AI 使用指南
- AI is a tool to enhance creativity, not replace it
- Always add your own ideas and improvements to AI output
- Never present AI-generated content as entirely your own without acknowledgment
- Be respectful in all interactions with AI (practice good habits)

### Classroom Expectations / 课堂期望
- Be creative and have fun
- Help your classmates when they get stuck
- It's okay to make mistakes — that's how we learn
- Ask questions whenever something is unclear

---

## Final Project Options / 毕业项目选项

Students choose one of the following for their capstone project (or propose their own):

1. **AI Magazine** — A multi-page digital magazine with AI-written articles and AI-generated illustrations
2. **AI Short Film** — An expanded slideshow film with original story, visuals, and soundtrack
3. **AI Game** — A more complex interactive game with multiple features
4. **Free Choice** — Any creative project that combines at least 2 AI tools
